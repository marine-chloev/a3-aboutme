about me

Hi! my name is

Marine

and one thing you should know about me is that I am very proud of my name!

“Marine” is a French name with Latin origins, meaning “from the sea” ,

which leads me to my next point. I am half French and half Cambodian—a heritage I’m still learning about because I was born and raised in Toronto, Canada.

I’m getting sidetracked here, but the main point:

"Marine" is a very meaningful word to me.

It’s a name that ties together my different identities, which is why I try to incorporate it into my work.

Through my creations, you get to know me on a personal level. Look for all the aquatic themes in my work—maybe you’ll discover something you didn’t notice the first time.
